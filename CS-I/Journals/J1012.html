1.1:
Starting at the value of the 1st number's least significant figure, increase its value by the 2nd number's least significant figure. Continue this and repeat with the next significant figures in order. If the value of 2 numerals increase to more than the base, remove the value equivalent to the base from the result and add 1 to the sum of the next sigificant figure's addition.

2.1:
In an octal system the base is 8, meaning that the largest figure is 7, and when the addends sum up to larger than 7 you must carry a 1 over

3.1:
In hexadecimal, the base is 16 so the largest figure is F (value of 15). You must not carry over until the value of the sum is larger than F (15)

4.1:
In binary the base is 2, meaning the value of each place is either 0 or 1. Addition of any 2 figures with value will always result in a carry over into the next place.
